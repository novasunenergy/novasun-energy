Who We Are

NovaSun Energy & Services is a trusted Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction) company based in Nashik, Maharashtra. Founded by Vivek Patil, we specialize in high-quality solar power systems.

Our mission is to help you dramatically reduce electricity bills and contribute to a cleaner, greener future through highly reliable solar technology.
